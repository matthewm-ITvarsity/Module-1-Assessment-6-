PLAYSTATION 5

Price :R11 999

Stock Avaiability in SA

 
     

 
      Orders for the March stock drop of PlayStation 5 (PS5) have opened at local distributor Koodoo.
      The retailer announced shortly after noon on 16 March that orders for the latest wave of stock were now open.
      This includes the standard PS5, the PS5 Digital Edition, as well as a PS5 Plus Bundle.
      The PS5 Plus Bundle includes the PlayStation 5 console, a 90-day PSN subscription, an additional controller, and two PS5 games.
      The two games are Marvel’s Spider-Man: Miles Morales Ultimate Edition and Devil May Cry Special Edition.
      Globally, stock of the PS5 has been extremely limited

Description

 
     

      The next generation of PlayStation is finally here with the arrival of the PS5. This generation, it seems like Sony is sticking with the approach that
       made the PS4 so successful: sell consoles that can play first-party games from Sony’s storied franchises, and supplement that lineup with great third-party titles, too.
       You should expect to see better graphics in your games, and the PS5 will also support high refresh rates, which should make games feel smoother.
       Plus, the PS5’s custom SSD promises to offer such a leap forward in loading speeds that it could change the way games are designed.
